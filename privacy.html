PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: December 2024

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Win 10min, we believe in complete transparency about data collection. We've designed our app to respect your privacy from the ground up.

What We DON'T Collect

Win 10min does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses (unless you contact support)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Browsing history
  • App usage data from other apps
  • Any data from blocked applications

What Stays on Your Device

All your Win 10min data is stored locally on your device:

  • Focus session history
  • City progress and buildings
  • Achievement data
  • Statistics and streaks
  • Custom settings and preferences
  • Your chosen username

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Screen Time Integration

Win 10min uses iOS Screen Time features to block apps. This integration:

  • Operates entirely through Apple's secure APIs
  • Does not give us access to your Screen Time data
  • Cannot see which specific apps you use
  • Only blocks apps during active focus sessions

In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed through the App Store:

  • Apple handles all payment processing
  • We never see your payment information
  • Purchase validation occurs through Apple's servers
  • Subscription status is stored locally on your device

Analytics

We use minimal, privacy-focused analytics to improve the app:

  • Basic app performance metrics (crashes, load times)
  • Anonymous usage patterns (which features are popular)
  • No personally identifiable information
  • No tracking across apps or websites

Data Export

Premium users can export their data as CSV files. This export:

  • Creates a file stored locally on your device
  • Contains only your focus session history
  • Is never automatically sent anywhere
  • Remains under your complete control

Third-Party Services

Win 10min uses these third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and payments
  • CloudKit (Optional): For iCloud sync between your devices (if enabled)

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data anytime (Premium feature)
  • Disable iCloud sync in Settings
  • Contact us to answer any privacy questions

Children's Privacy

Win 10min is designed for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of any material changes to this privacy policy through the app. Continued use of Win 10min after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
